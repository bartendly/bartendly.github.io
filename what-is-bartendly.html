Why Bartendly exists

Cocktail events should not disappear in Stories.

Guest shifts, takeovers, masterclasses and brand activations often disappear inside Instagram stories, flyers and private chats.

Bartendly gives them a place to be discovered, connected to bars and bartenders, and turned into real hospitality opportunities.

  • Find what is happening tonight or this week.
  • Discover bars, bartenders and events by city.
  • Add your own events and become visible to the scene.

The simple version

Bartendly makes the cocktail world visible and connected.

Cocktail events are everywhere, but they are scattered across Instagram stories, flyers, WhatsApp groups and private networks. Bartendly gives them one structured place.

Each event can connect the host bar, guest bartender, participating brand, city and date. That creates more than a calendar: it creates a living map of the cocktail scene.

Bartendly Experiences is the service layer of that ecosystem, helping clients hire professional bartenders for real-world events.

From isolated flyers to connected hospitality data. Events create the links. The links create discovery, visibility and future services.
Why it matters

Cocktail events should not disappear after one night.

A guest shift, a takeover or a masterclass is not just a post. It says which bar is active, which bartender is travelling, which brand is investing and which city has energy.

When this information becomes structured, it becomes useful: for guests choosing where to go, for professionals building visibility, for bars attracting traffic and for brands understanding the scene.

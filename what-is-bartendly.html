Why Bartendly exists

Cocktail events should not disappear in Stories.

Guest shifts, takeovers, masterclasses and brand activations often disappear inside Instagram stories, flyers and private chats.

Bartendly gives them a place to be discovered, connected to bars, bartenders and brands, and turned into real hospitality opportunities.

  • Find what is happening tonight, this week or while travelling.
  • Discover bars, bartenders, brands and events with context.
  • Add your own events and become visible to the cocktail scene.

The bigger idea

Bartendly connects events, bars, bartenders and brands.

The cocktail world is vibrant but fragmented. Bartendly creates a structured place where people, venues, events and brands become visible, connected and useful over time.

Events first

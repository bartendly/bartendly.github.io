Bartender Professional Conditions

1) Parties & Status

These Professional Conditions apply between:

  • Bartendly OÜ (“Bartendly”), acting as a commercial principal and coordination platform, and
  • The Bartender (“Professional”), acting as an independent contractor.

Nothing in these Professional Conditions creates an employment relationship, partnership, agency, joint venture, or exclusivity between Bartendly and the Professional.

The Professional acts independently and remains fully responsible for their own professional, legal, tax, and social obligations.

2) Scope of Collaboration

Bartendly may offer the Professional opportunities to provide bartending services for private or corporate events. The Professional is free to accept or decline any proposed assignment.

Once an assignment is accepted and confirmed, the Professional commits to delivering the agreed services in accordance with the assignment details communicated by Bartendly.

The Professional does not represent Bartendly toward clients and has no authority to bind Bartendly contractually.

3) Booking & Assignment Confirmation

An assignment is considered confirmed only when:

  • the client has paid the required deposit or full amount to Bartendly, and
  • Bartendly has explicitly confirmed the assignment to the Professional (in writing).

The Professional must not contact the client directly before assignment confirmation, unless explicitly authorized by Bartendly.

4) Professional Obligations

The Professional commits to:

  • arrive on time and remain for the agreed duration,
  • perform services with professional skill, care, and diligence,
  • comply with applicable laws, venue rules, and client instructions,
  • behave responsibly, respectfully, and professionally at all times,
  • refrain from consuming alcohol during service unless explicitly agreed in writing.

4.1 Professional Representations. The Professional represents and warrants that:

  • all information provided to Bartendly regarding skills, experience, certifications, and background is accurate and not misleading,
  • they are legally entitled to perform the assigned services in the relevant jurisdiction.

The Professional must not present themselves as an employee, partner, agent, or legal representative of Bartendly, and may not make commitments, representations, or statements on behalf of Bartendly.

Bartendly enforces zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, violence, theft, or unsafe conduct.

4.2) Safe & Respectful Working Conditions

Bartendly is committed to ensuring that Professionals operate in a safe, respectful, and professional environment.

The Professional shall not be required to tolerate any form of harassment, abusive behavior, sexual language, discrimination, intimidation, or unsafe conditions from clients, guests, or third parties.

If such behavior occurs, the Professional shall inform Bartendly as soon as reasonably possible.

In cases where the Professional’s safety, dignity, or well-being is compromised, the Professional is entitled to suspend or leave the assignment without penalty or blame, provided Bartendly is notified.

Such suspension or departure shall not be considered a breach of these Professional Conditions.

5) Scope & Change Management

The Professional must deliver only the services agreed for the assignment.

Any request for changes (menu, ingredients, duration, service level, dress code, or scope) must be referred to Bartendly.

The Professional has no authority to negotiate pricing, scope, or commercial terms with the client.

6) Substitution & Cancellation by the Professional

The Professional must notify Bartendly immediately if they become unavailable.

Substitution by the Professional is not permitted without Bartendly’s prior written approval.

Late cancellation or no-show may result in:

  • immediate termination of collaboration,
  • exclusion from future assignments,
  • liability for direct damages caused to Bartendly (including client claims or replacement costs).
7) Payment, Pricing & Taxation

7.1 Net Pricing. All fees agreed between Bartendly and the Professional are net amounts, meaning:

  • the agreed fee represents the final net remuneration received by the Professional,
  • the fee is deemed to include all applicable taxes, social contributions, and VAT (if any).

7.2 Payment Method.

  • The Professional is paid exclusively by Bartendly, never by the client.
  • Payments are made by bank transfer or equivalent method.
  • The Professional must provide accurate bank account details for payment purposes.

7.3 Invoicing (Optional).

  • If the Professional operates through a registered company or as a registered freelancer, they may issue an invoice to Bartendly.
  • If VAT is legally required, the Professional may include VAT on the invoice at their own responsibility.

Important: Regardless of invoicing or VAT status, the agreed price remains net. Any VAT or tax is deemed included in the agreed amount and does not increase the total payable by Bartendly.

7.4 Tax Responsibility. The Professional is fully and solely responsible for:

  • declaring their income,
  • paying any applicable income tax, VAT, or social contributions,
  • complying with all local tax and regulatory obligations.

Bartendly does not provide tax advice and bears no responsibility for the Professional’s tax situation.

8) Expenses

Only expenses explicitly approved in advance by Bartendly are reimbursable.

The Professional must not incur costs on behalf of Bartendly without prior written approval.

9) Liability & Insurance

The Professional remains fully responsible for their own actions, omissions, conduct, and compliance with applicable laws during assignments.

The Professional shall indemnify and hold harmless Bartendly from any claims, damages, losses, liabilities, or expenses arising from:

  • on-site performance,
  • misconduct or negligence,
  • breach of professional obligations,
  • violation of laws, venue rules, or client instructions.

Bartendly is not responsible for on-site supervision, alcohol consumption or guest behavior, or execution of the bartending service itself.

The Professional is encouraged to maintain appropriate professional liability insurance.

10) Confidentiality, Non-Circumvention & Non-Solicitation

All client identities, contact details, event information, pricing, and commercial terms introduced by Bartendly are confidential.

The Professional agrees that they shall not, directly or indirectly, contract with, provide services to, consult for, advise, solicit, or accept work from any client, company, venue, or intermediary introduced by Bartendly, without Bartendly’s prior written consent.

This restriction applies during the collaboration and for a period of twenty-four (24) months after the last assignment involving the relevant client.

Circumvention includes, but is not limited to:

  • working directly with the client,
  • working through a third party, agency, or intermediary,
  • referring the client to another bartender or entity outside Bartendly.

In case of breach, Bartendly reserves the right to:

  • terminate collaboration immediately,
  • claim damages equal to lost platform fees or commissions,
  • exclude the Professional from future opportunities.

Bartendly may reference the Professional’s name, profile, and professional background internally or for client presentation purposes related to assignments. Such use is limited to operational, commercial, and coordination needs and does not imply exclusivity or employment.

11) Data Protection (GDPR)

Bartendly collects and processes the Professional’s personal data solely for:

  • assignment coordination,
  • compliance purposes,
  • payment processing (including bank account details).

Data is processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR.

Bartendly does not sell or misuse personal data and limits access to what is strictly necessary for operations.

12) Termination

Bartendly may terminate collaboration at any time in case of serious breach.

The Professional may stop collaborating at any time, subject to honoring confirmed assignments.

Termination or suspension of an assignment under Section 4.2 (Safe & Respectful Working Conditions) shall not constitute a breach by the Professional.

13) Governing Law

These Professional Conditions are governed by the laws applicable to Bartendly OÜ, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

In case of interpretation issues, the English version of these Professional Conditions shall prevail.

Acceptance of an assignment implies full acceptance of these Professional Conditions.

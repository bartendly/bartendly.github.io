Privacy Policy
Updated: July 2025
1. Information We Collect

Bartendly collects only the minimum information needed to connect you with bartenders and process your requests, such as:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone/WhatsApp)
  • Event details (date, location, type of event)
  • Any info you voluntarily provide in your booking request
2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Facilitate connections between clients and bartenders
  • Send quotes, confirmations, and event communications
  • Improve our platform and customer experience
  • Comply with legal obligations
3. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. We only share data with bartenders or partners necessary to fulfill your request, or as required by law.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to keep your data secure. Only authorized staff and service providers can access your personal information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To make a request, email martial@bartendly.com

6. Cookies & Analytics

Bartendly uses minimal cookies for essential website operation. We may use basic analytics to improve the site, but do not track users for advertising.

7. Changes to this Policy

Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page. Continued use of the site means you accept the updated policy.

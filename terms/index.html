1) Platform Role

Bartendly is a platform connecting clients with independent bartenders for private and corporate events. Bartendly does not organize, supervise, employ, or directly provide bartending services. The service relationship for performance at the event exists between the client and the independent bartender(s).

Bartendly may act as a billing and payment agent on behalf of independent bartenders. By using the platform, clients and bartenders authorize Bartendly to issue invoices and collect payments. This agency role does not change the independent status of bartenders.

2) Definitions
  • Client: the person or company booking bartending services.
  • Bartender: an independent professional providing bartending services via Bartendly.
  • Booking: a confirmed engagement for a specific date, time, and location.
  • Deposit: 50% of the total price (private) or as specified for corporate bookings.
3) Quotes, Booking & Payments (General)

Custom (“sur mesure”) pricing. Every quote is tailored to the client’s needs, considering bartender rates, menu design, supplier costs (e.g., ice, glassware, logistics), and transportation where applicable.

Payment methods: card (Stripe) and bank transfer (Wise / DBS Hong Kong). Prices include Bartendly’s 15% coordination fee and payment processor fees unless specified otherwise.

By confirming a booking or making any payment, the client accepts these Terms and the refund policies in the relevant section below.

4) Private Clients — Deposit, Balance & Refunds

Deposit: a 50% deposit of the total event price is required to confirm any private booking. This deposit secures the date and allows Bartendly to begin coordination work, including staff scheduling, supplier booking, and event preparation.

Refund policy: this 50% deposit is non-refundable once the booking has been confirmed, as it covers the administrative and preparatory work started immediately after confirmation. However, if a private client cancels within 48 hours of booking confirmation and more than 14 calendar days before the event, Bartendly may, at its discretion, refund part of the deposit minus its 15% coordination fee and payment processor fees.

Balance: the remaining 50% must be paid no later than 7 calendar days before the event. Once paid, this balance becomes non-refundable as it is used to compensate bartenders and confirmed suppliers. In some local cases, the post-event balance may be settled directly with the bartender; any such arrangement must be agreed in writing with Bartendly.

5) Corporate Clients — Payment & Refunds

Payment: by default, corporate bookings require 100% upfront payment to confirm the event (unless different terms are agreed in writing for approved partners).

Refund policy (calendar days):

  • ≥ 14 days before event: refund of total amount minus Bartendly’s 15% platform fee and payment processor fees.
  • 14–7 days before event: 50% refund of total amount minus Bartendly’s 15% platform fee and payment processor fees.
  • < 7 days before event: no refund (bartenders and suppliers already confirmed).

Bartendly or bartender cancellation: Bartendly will make every reasonable effort to provide an equivalent replacement at no additional cost. If no suitable replacement can be arranged, 100% of all payments received will be refunded.

  • Communicate requirements, preferences, and constraints clearly prior to the event.

8) Replacement & Complaints

If a confirmed bartender becomes unavailable, Bartendly will attempt to replace them with an equivalent professional at no extra cost to the client. If a replacement is not possible, payments received will be refunded as stated in sections 4 and 5.

Any issue must be reported to billing@bartendly.com within 48 hours after the event. Bartendly will review the case and facilitate communication to help reach a fair resolution or goodwill remedy where appropriate.

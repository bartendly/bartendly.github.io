Why Bartenders Deserve Better Tools — and How Bartendly is Building Them

Let’s be honest: bartenders are some of the most talented professionals in hospitality — yet way too many of us remain invisible online.

We bring the energy, the craft, the vibes. We turn a quiet evening into a moment guests remember. But when it comes to getting booked for events or finding cool gigs — it’s often a mess of WhatsApp groups, random DMs, or awkward emails.

💻 Time for a smarter system

At Bartendly, we believe bartenders deserve their own digital space. A place to showcase their style, get noticed, and connect with real opportunities — without relying on middlemen or platforms that don’t get our world.

That’s why we’re building a commission-free platform (for now) that connects bartenders directly with private clients and bars — starting in London and Hong Kong.

🎯 What you can already do

  • ✅ Get featured on our public page
  • ✅ Respond to real event requests
  • ✅ Share your Instagram or WhatsApp directly
  • ✅ Keep 100% of your fee (no hidden cut)

All you need is a short bio, a photo, and your preferred contact info. We take care of the visibility.

🌍 What’s coming next

This is just the beginning. Bartendly is growing into a full platform made for you:

  • 📲 A mobile app to manage bookings
  • 🌟 Reviews and performance stats
  • 🤝 Brand deals and ambassador programs
  • 🌎 Job boards for gigs worldwide
  • 👀 More visibility than ever

We’re not here to “Uberize” the job. We’re here to make you visible, booked, and valued — on your terms.

🔑 Why now?

There’s never been a better time to reclaim your independence as a bartender. With Bartendly, you control how and when you work — and get discovered by people who respect the craft.

Whether you’re looking for extra gigs, long-term roles, or just a place to show your skills — we’ve got your back.

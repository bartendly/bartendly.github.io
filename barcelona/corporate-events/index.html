What we clarify before quoting

The goal is to avoid surprises: the right number of bartenders, the right service window and a realistic setup for the venue.

  • Event date, venue, access time and service hours
  • Guest count, expected peak moments and service style
  • Number of bartenders, profiles and dress code expectations
  • Alcohol, ice, glassware, tools, menu and supplier responsibilities

Staffing-first service

Bartendly mainly coordinates professional bartenders. Alcohol, stock, glassware, ice and the physical bar are usually handled by the client, venue, caterer or production team. For selected projects, cocktail menu or supplier support can be discussed separately.

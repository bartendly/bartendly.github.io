How we prepare

The best service is decided before the first drink.

We clarify the practical details that make the difference: timing, access, guest flow, bartender profile, service scope and what is already provided on site.

Trade-show setup

Built around the stand, not around a generic party.

At a fair, service has to work within fixed hours, limited space, visitor peaks and professional expectations. The bartender should support the conversation, not interrupt it.

The brief

A few details are enough to understand the service level, team size and bartender profile needed.

  • City, venue or stand location
  • Date, timing and setup constraints
  • Guest flow, event tone and expected volume

The team fit

The bartender profile depends on the setting: fast and precise for trade shows, discreet for private homes, polished for rooftops and brand receptions.

The proposal

A practical recommendation for team size, timing, scope and next steps. If the request is not realistic, we say so early.

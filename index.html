Clear service scope

Bartendly Experiences is primarily a bartending service coordination layer. We usually do not provide alcohol. In most cases, alcohol is handled by the client, venue, stand supplier or event production team. Ice, glassware or tools can sometimes be discussed, but the cleanest setup is bartending service only.

Connected to World of Mixologists

Bartendly is connected to WØM, a global cocktail culture platform followed by bars, bartenders and hospitality professionals worldwide. It strengthens our access to the cocktail scene without distracting from the service: getting the right bartenders for your event.
